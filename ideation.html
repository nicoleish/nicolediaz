  • Creative Process Video Documentary

    hayao miyazaki has created so many amazing films and our team wanted to explore his process



    • As a team of 3, we created a video documenting the creative process of Hayao Miyazaki. This was not a “documentary” in the traditional sense, the video both documents your chosen creator’s process, and is also an experimental video work in and of itself. It's about telling the story of how he came to be an artist. It's about our team telling the story of his process, without a linear narrative.
